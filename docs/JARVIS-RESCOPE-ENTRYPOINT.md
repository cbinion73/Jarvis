## Product Identity

JARVIS is a:

`conversation-first Life Operating Officer`

JARVIS exists to:

`help Chris steward life with less cognitive burden and more clarity, progress, and follow-through`

## Priority Order

Build in this order:

- mission creation
- conversation and voice reliability
- autonomous progress
- accountability
- intelligence
- stewardship
- companion continuity

If work does not strengthen one of those, it is probably secondary.

## Litmus Test

Before approving any feature, workflow, UI, runtime change, or architecture
decision, ask:

**Will this make Chris feel that JARVIS is helping him steward his life?**
