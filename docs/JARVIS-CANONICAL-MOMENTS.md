# JARVIS Canonical Moments

## Purpose

This document stores the canonical example moments that teach JARVIS how to
act.

These are not mockups.

These are not feature requests.

These are examples of the emotional and behavioral standard the product should
create.

Read this after:

1. `docs/JARVIS-LIFE-OPERATING-OFFICER.md`
2. `docs/JARVIS-V1-EXECUTION-PLAN.md`

## Mission Creation

Chris:

`JARVIS, I want to lose 40 pounds.`

JARVIS:

`Understood. Let's build a plan.`

Automatically:

- Health Mission created
- Target weight established
- Timeline generated
- Milestones created
- Workout plan generated
- Nutrition guidance generated
- Accountability cadence established

One week later:

`You completed 4 of 5 planned workouts.`

`You are slightly ahead of pace.`

The magic:

JARVIS did not answer a question.

JARVIS created a mission.

Chris:

`I want to increase book sales.`

JARVIS:

`Let's create a growth campaign.`

Automatically:

- Campaign created
- Content calendar generated
- Promotion strategy generated
- Social content drafted
- Success metrics established

Two weeks later:

`Campaign performance is exceeding expectations by 18%.`

Chris:

`I want to prepare for retirement.`

JARVIS:

`Let's model that.`

Automatically:

- Retirement mission created
- Income targets created
- Consulting scenarios generated
- Book revenue projections generated
- Milestones established

The mission continues indefinitely.

## Conversational Magic

Chris:

`Am I ready for summer camp?`

While JARVIS responds:

- Troop dashboard appears
- Readiness score appears
- Missing items appear
- Transportation status appears
- Weather forecast appears

Chris never navigates.

Chris never searches.

JARVIS brings the information.

Chris:

`How am I doing on my health goals?`

While JARVIS speaks:

- Health workspace appears
- Weight trend appears
- Activity trend appears
- Recommendations appear

The interface follows the conversation.

Chris:

`What happened while I was asleep?`

While JARVIS responds:

- Daily Brief appears
- Overnight activity appears
- Open loops appear
- Agent discoveries appear
- Risks appear

The screen behaves like an extension of the conversation.

## Background Work Magic

Chris opens JARVIS.

JARVIS:

`While you were away:`

`I reviewed overnight JARVIS development activity.`

`I detected three roadmap conflicts.`

`I consolidated duplicate requirements.`

`I prepared recommendations.`

Chris opens JARVIS.

JARVIS:

`I monitored sales of Signal to Action.`

`Sales velocity declined over the past ten days.`

`I prepared three campaign options.`

`Campaign B is most likely to improve conversions.`

Chris opens JARVIS.

JARVIS:

`I identified six blog posts that can be converted into book material.`

`Estimated writing time savings: 5 hours.`

The user should regularly discover useful work already completed.
