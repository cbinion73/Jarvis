New Direction

Calm command presence, not a dashboard.

This redesign treats JARVIS as an always-on Life Operating Officer. The interface centers conversation, mission stewardship, and trustable background work. The MCU inspiration is not the flashy HUD itself, but the feeling that JARVIS already understands the objective and quietly brings the right system into view.
