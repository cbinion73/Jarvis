Why this is different

The current Glass UI is memorable, but it buries the model of the product. This version exposes what JARVIS actually manages.

  • Objects first, not scenes first
  • One decision queue instead of scattered urgency
  • MUI-style density, chips, cards, and data tables
  • Glass retained as a material layer, not the main story

Visual Direction
Keep Glass, soften spectacle
Retain translucency, depth, and calm polish from Glass, but shift the emphasis to readable cards, stronger hierarchy, stable tables, and explicit actions. The visual tone moves from cinematic sci-fi dashboard toward trustworthy operational console.
